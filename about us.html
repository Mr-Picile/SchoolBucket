About the Hunger-Be-Gone

The Hunger-Be-Gone combines new technology and traditional cooking to produce a delicious and effective solution to hunger.

Here's how it works: Every Hunger-Be-Gone is specially made to fit an average 2,400 pound cow. The Hunger-Be-Gone is then sent up to a Hungree-Corp satelite. When it is time for deployment, the Hunger-Be-Gone detatches from the satelite and flys towards its designated location. The Hunger-Be-Gone is made specifically without atmospheric sheilding so the cow cooks on its way down. This technique ensures a flavorfull, well-done meal every time.
